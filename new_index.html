wwwegetables

wwwelcome, dear friend!

wwwegetables are a student-run project with an interest in plants and networks. we organize collective dinners and a shared garden at hfbk hamburg.

dig in with us at
the dinners

which happen on wednesdays at the kochwerkstatt of the filmbuilding (finkenau 42). they are open to students, staff, family, friends, and generally those who could enjoy a lovely wwweggie meal.

dig in with us in
the garden

which is a great place (right next to the filmbuilding) to meet various new friends. here you can meet a plethora of insects, snails, birds, plant some wwweggies or flowers, ponder, photosynthesize, and so much more.
