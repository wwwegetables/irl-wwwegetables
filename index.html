wwwhy do we exist?

wwwegetables are a student-organized project with a curious eye for plants and networks. their purpose is to build a sustainable community practice by organizing and tending to the third space, shared garden and collective dinners.the project sees community as an ebb and flow of participants* and energy, at times it is a polyphony of ideas and visitors, and at other times — *crickets* and hibernation. that is why wwwegetables are focused on nurturing the formed connections and reaching out to the new ones.

wwwhere are we?

we are based at hfbk hamburg, an art school in northern germany. we meet to eat in the kochwerkstatt/kitchen of the filmhaus on campus. (cute little map coming soon!) the garden is right by the kitchen door, so sometimes we can eat outside.
