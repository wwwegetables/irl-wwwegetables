wwwhy?

wwwegetables are a student-organized project with a curious eye for plants and networks. their purpose is to build a sustainable community practice by tending to a shared garden and hosting collective dinners. the project sees community as an ebb and flow of participants* and energy, at times it is a polyphony of ideas and visitors, and at other times — *crickets* and hibernation. that is why wwwegetables are focused on nurturing the formed connections and reaching out to the new ones.

wwwhere?

we meet & eat at hfbk hamburg in the kochwerkstatt, located in the filmhaus. (cute little map coming soon!) the garden is right by the kitchen door, so sometimes we can eat outside.
